Forest School

Nature is an extension of the classroom. We go outside to explore, move, observe and learn from experience.

In the forest, real skills develop: coordination, balance, self-regulation, curiosity, cooperation and confidence. Learning happens spontaneously when the environment invites investigation.

We accompany these experiences with proposals appropriate to each age and with a respectful pace: listening, looking, touching, asking and discovering.
